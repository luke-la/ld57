Welcome to SINK.

The goal of the game is to navigate as far down the oil rig as you can.

Be careful, because the coral (the red sections) can cut your air cable.

There is no set ending yet, but if you make it down past 100m you've won!

Controls:

  • Press Play to start!
  • A and D to move left and right
  • W and S to move forward and back
  • Space Bar to jump
  • T to weld an anchor your tether
